Strongman

Strongman is a strenght building methodology that is based on functional strength. The modern sport stems from a long history of men competing in lifting, and moving large heavy objects. There are many varied events in a strongman competition including the following examples:

  • Atlas Stones
  • Axle Press
  • Car Flip
  • Conan's Wheel
  • Deadlift
  • Deadlift Hold
  • Dumbbell Press
  • Farmers Walk
  • Fingal's Fingers
  • Frame Carry
  • Hercules Hold
  • Husafell Stone
  • Keg Toss
  • Loading Race
  • Log Clean and Press
  • Power Stairs
  • Squat
  • Tire Flip
  • Vehicle Pull
  • Yoke Carry

Together, these events test strength across many different variables such as: grip strength, core strength, explosive strength, and overall full body power.
