Project Proposal Non-Denominational Strength

Objectives:

The goal of this website is to create a one stop place for information on various strength building methodologies. There are many different methods of getting stronger, and this site hopes to give a good starting point for anyone just starting out or wanting to explore a different methodology than they have previously experienced. The initial offering will include information on the following:

  • Powerlifting
  • Olympic Weightlifting
  • Stong Man
  • Crossfit
  • Body Building

Target Audience:

  • Both males and females 18+
  • Anyone with a desire to gain physical strength or learn about strength gaining methodologies
  • Assume a wide range of technical savvy of users
  • Mobile device friendly

Site Type:

This website will mainly serve as an online informational brochure. Realizing that this website cannont immediately catch up to the overwhelming amount of information already on the Internet relating to strength training, it will likely feature links to other sites with more detail in specific methodologies or forums where these methodologies are being discussed.
