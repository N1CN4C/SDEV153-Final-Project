Crossfit

Crossfit is a relatively new addition to the world of strength training methodologies. From its creation, it has set out to define fitness in the broadest sense, so it is not exclusively about strength training. Their definition of fiteness is increased work capacity across broad time and modal domains. Their training method is based on constantly varied functional movements performed at high intensity. The following is a list of strength specific movements used in Crossfit:

  • Squat
  • Deadlift
  • Overhead Press
  • Jerk
  • Push Press
  • Clean
  • Snatch
  • Yolk Carry
  • Sled Push

As you can see, many of these are lifts or movements that are used in other strength training methodologies. Crossfit aims to do the oposite of specialization in order to create athletes as well rounded as possible. This list is also not comprehensive since Crossfit is constantly adding new and more varied exercises.
